# Pomodoro Focus Timer Guide

## How to Run Locally
1. Open the project folder in your computer.
2. Double-click on `index.html` to open the website in any modern web browser.
3. Click on the **Focus Timer** card on the homepage to start using the tool.

## How to Use
- **Start/Pause**: Click the large **Start** button or press the **Spacebar**.
- **Reset**: Click the **Reset** button or press the **R** key to restart the current session.
- **Skip**: Click the skip icon (next to Reset) or press the **S** key to move to the next session immediately (no EXP awarded).

## Changing Durations
- Scroll down to the **Settings** section within the Focus Timer tool.
- Enter your preferred minutes for **Focus**, **Short Break**, and **Long Break**.
- **Note**: Changes will apply automatically to the *next* session that starts.

## Resetting Statistics
- In the **EXP Rewards** panel on the right (or bottom on mobile), click the **Reset Stats** button.
- This will clear your session counts and total focus minutes, but your **Global Total EXP** will be preserved.

## EXP System
- **Focus Session (25 min)**: +10 EXP
- **Full Set (4 Focus Sessions)**: +20 Bonus EXP
- All EXP earned is added to your global student profile!
